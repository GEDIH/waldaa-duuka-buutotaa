About WDB Management System

Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church Membership Management

The WDB Management System is designed to help Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church communities manage their membership, contributions, and administrative tasks efficiently.

Key Features

Member Management

Member registration and management

Contribution Tracking

Contribution tracking and reporting

Administration

Administrative dashboard and tools

Multi-Language

Multi-language support (English and Oromo)

Our Mission

To provide Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church communities with modern, efficient tools for managing their membership and administrative needs while preserving cultural and linguistic heritage.

Technology

  • • Responsive design
  • • Multi-language support

Backend Technologies

  • • MySQL database

  • • Secure authentication
